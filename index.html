About


Mohammad Asif Khan is a Multimedia Journalist based in New Delhi.


He holds a degree in Convergent Journalism from AJK-MCRC, Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. Currently, he is employed at "Force Magazine," where he specializes in defense news and national security coverage. His primary focus is on reporting security issues and conflicts across South Asia, with a particular interest in cross-border stories.


In addition to his work at "Force Magazine," Asif also collaborates and freelances with various Indian and international publications for special stories. Some of the publications he's worked with include TRT World, Mondoweiss, Middle East Monitor, BBC World Service, Article 14, Maktoob Media, PARI Network, and The Citizen.


Employing different formats such as video, audio, and written mediums, Asif strives to effectively convey information, perspectives, and stories that deserve to be told. His work is a testament to his commitment to journalism and his passion for telling impactful stories
