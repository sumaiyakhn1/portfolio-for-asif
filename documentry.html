Fading designs of Bhadohi


The documentary explores the intricate world of carpet weaving through the lens of a seasoned artisan, whose journey intertwines personal experiences, familial influences, and industry challenges.

Set against the backdrop of Bhadohi, renowned for its rich tradition of carpet weaving, the narrative begins with the speaker's childhood, steeped in the vibrant ambiance of a handloom next door. Through keen observation, they absorb the nuances of the craft, a skill that becomes an integral part of their identity.
